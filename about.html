About FootData Lab

Exploring European Football Through Open Data

FootData Lab is a data-driven project focused on analyzing and visualizing information from the main European football leagues. Using open data sources, the platform provides insights into league standings, team performance, and historical trends in a clear and accessible way.

Our Mission

To make football statistics easy to understand and accessible by transforming open data into meaningful visual insights.

Our Vision

To become a reference platform for analyzing European football leagues using transparent and reliable open data.

Our Values

Accuracy, transparency, and clarity in data presentation, combined with a passion for football analytics.

Why FootData Lab

Clear, Reliable and Accessible Football Data

This project is designed to showcase how open data can be used to analyze real-world sports scenarios, combining web development and data visualization techniques.

  • Open and transparent data sources
  • Focus on major European leagues
  • Clean and intuitive visualizations
  • Academic and analytical approach
  • Built with modern web technologies
Explore the Data
FootData Lab

FootData Lab provides comprehensive insights into European football leagues and teams using open data. Explore detailed statistics, track league standings, analyze top-performing clubs, and access historical and current season data, all in one place. Our goal is to make football data accessible, informative, and engaging for fans, analysts, and researchers alike.
